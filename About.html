WHO WE ARE ?

Afkanerd is an idea, the idea
being"We code for the people".
The ideology evolvedfrom the believe in
human rights, freedom of speech and expression.

WHAT WE DO ?

We use modern technologies
to build sustainable solutions that
benefit the people. We primarily focus
on open-source software for health care,
education and communication. We build in the
open and encourage you to join us on our mission
to make the world better and more accessible.
